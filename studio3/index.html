Instructions

You will be randomly assigned if Player 1 or Player 2 gets to start the round first.
The Player rolls the die and adds up the numbers after each roll.
The Player may stop rolling at any time and end the turn.
If the player rolls one "A", it switches to the other Player.
The Player will lose all points for the turn when a two "A"s is rolled.
Player wins if they score 30 points or more.
