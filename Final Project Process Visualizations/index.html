Pig Card Game Project Visualization

DES 157A - Interactive Media II - Winter 2023

The pictures above shows the step by step process of the newly updated visual for my pig card game. This approach is to have a newly designed game where it focuses on the UX design for the game. Specifically, I focused on navigation throughout the game, and making buttons and the direction of the game rather more obvious to not make the user think. I wanted it to make a seamless experience where users could play and have fun.
